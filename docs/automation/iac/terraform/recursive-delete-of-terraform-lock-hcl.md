---
title: Recursive delete of .terraform.lock.hcl
---

## What

When working with Atlantis, or terraform in git, sometimes a `.terraform.lock.hcl` gets in and breaks things

## How

```shell
find ./ -type f -name ".terraform.lock.hcl" -exec rm {} +
```
